How EIC Works

Direct accountability. One principal, one point of accountability, one standard of delivery.

EIC is structured for direct accountability. Every engagement is led, reviewed, and delivered by Benjamin Eukel as principal. The scale of work EIC can deliver is enabled by purpose-built delivery infrastructure: automated test gates, tiered quality review, AI-augmented capacity for parallel workstreams, and subcontracted specialists engaged when an engagement requires expertise outside the core practice. The client never deals with an account manager, a junior consultant, or a handoff. One principal, one point of accountability, one standard of delivery.

I built EIC because I spent 25 years wanting cover from a boss and never getting it.
